Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2025

1. Introduction

BeReal Museum ("the Website") is a privacy-first, client-side web application designed to help users explore, analyze, and preserve their personal BeReal data in a visually rich and meaningful way.

Your privacy is a core principle of this project. BeReal Museum is intentionally built so that your data never leaves your device.

2. No Affiliation With BeReal

BeReal Museum is an independent, fan-made project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by BeReal. All references to BeReal are used solely to describe compatibility with user-exported data.

If this status changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

3. Data Collection

BeReal Museum does not collect, receive, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Specifically:

  • No uploaded files are sent to any server
  • No personal information is collected by the Website
  • No photos, notes, locations, timestamps, or friend data are transmitted externally
  • All data processing happens entirely within your web browser

4. Local Processing Only

When you import your BeReal data export (ZIP file):

  • The file is processed locally using in-browser JavaScript
  • All analysis, rendering, and visualization occur on your device
  • No network requests are made containing your data
  • Imported data exists only during your active browser session unless explicitly stored locally by your browser (e.g., through optional browser storage mechanisms)

5. Data Storage

By default:

  • BeReal Museum does not persist your data on any server
  • Imported data is cleared when you refresh or close the page
  • If your browser stores any temporary or cached data, this data remains fully under your control and can be cleared at any time using standard browser settings

6. Cookies, Analytics, and Tracking

BeReal Museum:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not use advertising technologies
  • Does not embed third-party trackers or pixels

Your activity on the Website is not monitored or logged.

7. Third-Party Libraries

The Website uses client-side, open-source libraries (such as ZIP processing and mapping libraries) solely to enable its features.

These libraries:

  • Run entirely in your browser
  • Do not collect or transmit personal data
  • Do not communicate with external servers using your content

8. Hosting Provider

BeReal Museum is hosted as a static website using GitHub Pages.

GitHub Pages may collect limited technical information (such as IP addresses or request logs) as part of standard website hosting. This data is handled according to GitHub's own Privacy Policy and is not accessed or controlled by BeReal Museum.

9. Children's Privacy

BeReal Museum is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. No data is knowingly collected from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the Website or its features.

Any updates will be posted on this page, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly.

11. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Website, you may contact the project maintainer via GitHub.

Summary: Your data stays on your device. We can't see it, we don't want to see it, and we've built this entire application to ensure we never will. Your privacy is not just a policy—it's the architecture.
